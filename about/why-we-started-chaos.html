Why we started Chaos

We wanted to produce a new unique collection of ethical jewellery that used a fresh approach, featuring recycled silver and gold from a known and certified source.

At the moment it is almost impossible to prove that silver or gold come from a good, clean and ethical mine.

We were positive that there had to be another way to produce jewellery, and we found it (eventually!) within the 'secrets' of jewellery production itself. It’s the original recycled product!
