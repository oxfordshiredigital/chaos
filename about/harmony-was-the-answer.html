'Harmony' was the answer

Just months before we started our search for a source of pure recycled metal, one precious metal factory took the enlightened decision not to use any newly-mined metals, which was perfect for us.

This American family-led company is the one we have teamed up with to exclusively bring their Harmony brand of recycled silver and gold to Britain. The brand contains no newly mined silver or gold and is independently certificated to be recycled.
