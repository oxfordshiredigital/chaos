Chaos

The new brand that’s rethinking precious jewellery by creating unique designs in silver, adding a luxury touch with 18ct gold, and completing the look using only ethically precious metals.

Our designer has created the unexpected; lines that tangle and intertwine, that move in and around each other, all possessing our own signature “chaotic” look.

Gold and silver have always been recycled, but rarely kept apart from newly mined metal, and it took a long search to lead us to the only real source of certificated ethical and recycled precious metal.

This new recycled metal was perfect for what we wanted to do: release a genuine collection for those who wanted something different and thoughtful.

Now we are proud to reveal what has taken over two years to create. To rethink jewellery and create just a little……Chaos!
