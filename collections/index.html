Chaos Designs

Chaos promises to be one of the most exciting recent jewellery innovations.

The combination of clean, contemporary designs with pure 100% recycled silver and gold is just what today’s enlightened jewellery buyers want!

The Chaos name sprang into life from the creation of one of the collection concepts.

Adding a touch of luxury with a hint of gold on every piece in the brand was essential, and was very much the catalyst for the original Chaos collection.

Thin strands of silver and gold in random layers became the inspiration for the first collection and it was this feature that hinted at the name that ultimately became our brand – Chaos.
