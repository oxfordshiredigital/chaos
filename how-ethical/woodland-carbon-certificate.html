The Woodland Trust: The Woodland Trust is the UK’s leading woodland conservation charity. It has 300,000 members and supporters.

Established in 1972, the Woodland Trust now has more than 1,000 sites in its carecovering approximately 20,000 hectares (50,000 acres). Access to its sites is free.

Further news can be found at www.woodlandtrust.org.uk.
